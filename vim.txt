NORMAL MODE ###
		
 Navigation :
 	k --- up 
 	j --- down
 	l --- right
 	h --- left
	ex: 9l --- 9 letter right
 	w --- next word locate first letter
 	b --- last word locate first letter
 	e --- next word loacte ended letter
 	ge --- previous word locate ended letter
 	0 --- start of line 0.
 	^ --- locate start which  line of text
 	_ --- move to paraghif head.
 	g_ --- locate end current line.
 	$ --- locate end which line of text
 	fx --- find x charakter next
 	Fx --- find x charakter previous
 	gg --- first line -- ]]
 	G --- ended line -- [[
 	nG --- go to n line (53G)
 	fgo --- search next go character in line
 	Fgo --- search old go character in line
 	; --- next search word.
 	, --- old search word.
 	[ --- go first line .
 	] --- go end line. 
 	{ --- go next near space line.
 	} --- go last near space line. 
 	/x --- search x from file forward
 	?x --- search x from file backward
 	
 Window :
 	zz --- center scroll current cursor locate
 	zb --- top scroll current cursor locate
 	zt --- bottom scroll current cursor locate
 	H --- move to top of screen
 	M --- move to middle of screen
 	L --- move to bottom of screen
 				
VISUAL MODE ###

	v  --- enter visual mode
	Shift + v --- mark currentline.
	 
	
COMMAND LINE MODE ####
	: --- enter command line mode.
	:qa! --- Close all files, abandon changes
	:w --- Save
	:wq / :x --- Save and close file
	:q --- Close file
	:q! --- Close file, abandon changes
	ZZ --- Save and quit
	ZQ --- Quit without checking changes Close file

EDITING MODE ####
	jk --- insert to normal mode.
	u --- undo
	Ctrl + r - redo
	i --- insert left. 
	a --- insert right.
	I --- begin line insert.
	A --- end line insert.
	o ---bottom enter.
	O --- up enter .
	x --- delete right.(7x)
	s --- delete letter and insert mode.
	dd --- delete line enterly.(13dd)
	d$ --- delete line.
	D --- delete line ritght to end of line.
	d^ --- delete current position to start line.
	ex::: d0 d$ c$ d0
	cc --- delete line and insert mode.
	dw --- delete right word.
	db --- delete left word.
	daw --- delete a word.
	das --- delete a sentense
	dab --- delete () insite charakter
	daB --- delet {} insite charakter.
	cw --- delete word and insert mode.
	dG --- delete below all code. 
	dg --- delete up all code.
	di --- delete '' or "" or `` in text delete. 
	ci --- delete '' or "" or `` in text delete and insert mode.
	y --- copy form visual mode. 
	yw --- copy word. 
	yy --- copy line. 
	p --- paste. 
	d/x --- cursore current position delete to x.
	Ctrl Shift Arrow Up / Down --- multiple cursore.
	
REPLACE MODE ###
	
	Shift + r --- enter replace mode
